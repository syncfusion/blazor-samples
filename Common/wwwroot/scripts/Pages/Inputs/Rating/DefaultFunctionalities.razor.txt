<ActionDescription>
<p>This sample demonstrates the default functionalities of the Rating component. The component allows users to provide a star rating or view other people's ratings on a numeric scale for services such as movies, applications, or products.</p>
    
    <p><b>Key Features Demonstrated:</b></p>
    <p>This example showcases the usage of the following properties in the Blazor Rating component:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><code>AllowReset</code> - Enable users to reset their rating.</li>
        <li><code>ReadOnly</code> - Display ratings without allowing modification.</li>
        <li><code>EnableSingleSelection</code> - Allow only single rating selection.</li>
        <li><code>ItemsCount</code> - Configure the number of rating items to display.</li>
        <li><code>Disabled</code> - Disable the rating component.</li>
    </ul>
</ActionDescription>
