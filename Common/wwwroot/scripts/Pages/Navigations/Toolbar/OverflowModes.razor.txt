<ActionDescription>
<p>This sample demonstrates available overflow modes in the Blazor Toolbar.</p>
    
    <p>Supported overflow modes (via <a target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="code" href="https://help.syncfusion.com/cr/blazor/Syncfusion.Blazor.Navigations.ToolbarItem.html#Syncfusion_Blazor_Navigations_ToolbarItem_Overflow" aria-label="Class reference of OverflowMode property in Toolbar">OverflowMode</a> property):</p>
    
    <ul>
        <li><code>Scrollable</code> - Default mode with left/right scroll arrows for excess items</li>
        <li><code>Popup</code> - Overflow items moved to popup container</li>
        <li><code>Extended</code> - Overflow items displayed in next toolbar row on expand icon click</li>
        <li><code>MultiRow</code> - Overflow items displayed as inline toolbar rows</li>
    </ul>
</ActionDescription>
